Fluid is physical.
Fluid exhibits Pressure and Location.
            Pressure is physical.
            Pressure can be Low or High.
                        Low is initial.
                        High is final.
            Location is physical.
            Location can be Contained or Leaking.
AC power supply is environmental and physical.
Lubricating oil is physical.
Body is physical.
Body exhibits Anchor.
            Anchor is physical.
            Anchor can be Stable or Vibrating.
Body consists of Pump, Housing, and Motor.
            Pump is physical.
            Pump exhibits Rotation speed.
                        Rotation speed is physical.
                        Rotation speed can be Stop or High.
            Pump consists of Distance ring, Shaft nut, Casing wear ring, Impeller eye, Wear ring, Impeller, Pump shaft, Pump bearing, Blade, and Bellmouth.
                        Distance ring is physical.
                        Shaft nut is physical.
                        Casing wear ring is physical.
                        Impeller eye is physical.
                        Wear ring is physical.
                        Impeller is physical.
                        Pump shaft is physical.
                        Pump bearing is physical.
                        Blade is physical.
                        Bellmouth is physical.
            Housing is physical.
            Housing consists of Discharge nozzle, Volute casing, Discharge flange, Suction flange, Packing gland, Packing strip, Casing, Centerline support, Delivery pipe, Seal, Suction pipe, Staffing box, Packing gland, and Shroud.
                        Discharge nozzle is physical.
                        Volute casing is physical.
                        Discharge flange is physical.
                        Suction flange is physical.
                        Packing gland is physical.
                        Packing strip is physical.
                        Casing is physical.
                        Centerline support is physical.
                        Delivery pipe is physical.
                        Seal is physical.

                                    Seal temperature is physical.
                                    Seal temperature can be Low or High.
                        Suction pipe is physical.
                        Staffing box is physical.
                        Packing gland is physical.
                        Shroud is physical.

                                    Bearing temperature is physical.
                                    Bearing temperature can be High or Low.
                        Rotor is physical.
                        Stator is physical.
Body support is physical.
Body support consists of Pump feet, Motor feet, Anchor bolt, and Protection cage.
            Pump feet is physical.
            Motor feet is physical.
            Anchor bolt is physical.
            Protection cage is physical.
Cooling water is environmental and physical.
Accelerate is physical.
Accelerate requires Pump.
Accelerate changes Pressure from Low to High.
Contain is physical.
Contain requires Housing.
Contain changes Location from Leaking to Contained.
Rotation is physical.
Rotation requires AC power supply and Motor.
Rotation changes Rotation speed from Stop to High.
Bearing cooling is physical.
Bearing cooling requires Lubricating oil.
Bearing cooling changes Bearing temperature from High to Low.
Supporting is physical.
Supporting requires Body support.
Supporting changes Anchor from Vibrating to Stable.
Seal cooling is physical.
Seal cooling requires Cooling water.
Seal cooling changes Seal temperature from High to Low.
