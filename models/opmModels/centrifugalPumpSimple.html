Water is physical.
Water exhibits Pressure.
            Pressure is physical.
            Pressure can be Low or High.
                        Low is initial.
                        High is final.
Centrifugal pump is physical.
Centrifugal pump consists of Shaft, Impeller, and Motor.
            Shaft is physical.
            Impeller is physical.
            Motor is physical.
Increase pressure is physical.
Increase pressure requires Centrifugal pump.
Increase pressure changes Pressure from Low to High.
