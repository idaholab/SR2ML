Fluid is physical.
Fluid exhibits Pressure and Location.
            Pressure is physical.
            Pressure can be Low or High.
                        Low is initial.
                        High is final.
            Location is physical.
            Location can be Contained or Leaking.
Steam flow control is physical.
Steam flow control consists of Overspeed trip, Governor, and Trip valve.
            Overspeed trip is physical.
            Governor is physical.
Body is physical.
Body exhibits Body anchor.
            Body anchor is physical.
            Body anchor can be Stable or Vibrating.
Body consists of Pump, Housing, and Turbine.
            Pump is physical.
            Pump exhibits Pump rotation speed.
                        Pump rotation speed is physical.
                        Pump rotation speed can be Stop or High.
            Pump consists of Distance ring, Shaft nut, Casing wear ring, Impeller eye, Wear ring, Impeller, Pump shaft, and Bearings.
                        Distance ring is physical.
                        Shaft nut is physical.
                        Casing wear ring is physical.
                        Impeller eye is physical.
                        Wear ring is physical.
                        Impeller is physical.
                        Impeller consists of 12 Blades.
                                    Blade is physical.
                        Pump shaft is physical.
                        Bearings is physical.
            Housing is physical.
            Housing consists of Discharge nozzle, Volute casing, Discharge flange, Suction flange, Packing gland, Packing strips, Casing, Centerline support, Delivery pipe, Seal, Suction pipe, and Staffing box.
                        Discharge nozzle is physical.
                        Volute casing is physical.
                        Discharge flange is physical.
                        Suction flange is physical.
                        Packing gland is physical.
                        Packing strips is physical.
                        Casing is physical.
                        Centerline support is physical.
                        Delivery pipe is physical.
                        Seal is physical.
                        Seal exhibits Seal temperature.
                                    Seal temperature is physical.
                                    Seal temperature can be Low or High.
                        Suction pipe is physical.
                        Staffing box is physical.
            Turbine is physical.
            Turbine exhibits Turbine rotation speed.
                        Turbine rotation speed is physical.
                        Turbine rotation speed can be Stop or High.
            Turbine consists of Turbine coupling, Bearings, Turbine impeller, and Turbine support body.
                        Turbine coupling is physical.
                        Bearings is physical.
                        Turbine impeller is physical.
                        Turbine impeller consists of 18 Blades.
                                    Blade is physical.
                        Turbine support body is physical.
Body supports is physical.
Body supports consists of Pump feet, Turbine feet, Pump anchor bolts, Turbine anchor bolts, and Protection cage.
            Pump feet is physical.
            Turbine feet is physical.
            Pump anchor bolts is physical.
            Protection cage is physical.
Component cooling water is environmental and physical.
Steam is physical.
Steam exhibits Steam pressure.
            Steam pressure is physical.
            Steam pressure can be Low or High.
                        Low is final.
Accelerate is physical.
Accelerate requires Pump.
Accelerate changes Pressure from Low to High.
Contain is physical.
Contain requires Housing.
Contain changes Location from Leaking to Contained.
Rotation is physical.
Rotation requires Turbine.
Rotation changes Pump rotation speed from Stop to High.
Steam expansion is physical.
Steam expansion requires Steam flow control.
Steam expansion changes Turbine rotation speed from Stop to High and Steam pressure from High to Low.
Supporting is physical.
Supporting requires Body supports.
Supporting changes Body anchor from Vibrating to Stable.
Seal cooling is physical.
Seal cooling requires Component cooling water.
Seal cooling changes Seal temperature from High to Low.
