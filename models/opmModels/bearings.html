Shaft is environmental and physical.
Shaft exhibits Rotation speed.
            Rotation speed is physical.
            Rotation speed can be Low or High.
Bearing is physical.
Bearing consists of Separator, Cage, Ball, Housing, Inner ring, and Outer ring.
            Separator is physical.
            Cage is physical.
            Ball is physical.
            Housing is physical.
            Inner ring is physical.
            Outer ring is physical.
Rotation is physical.
Rotation requires Bearing.
Rotation changes Rotation speed from Low to High.
